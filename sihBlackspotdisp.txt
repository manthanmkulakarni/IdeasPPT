Road accident deaths is a cause of major concern of highway.
Black spots are the place where historically accidents are concentrated.
So GIS can be used to reduce the accidents at black spots.
By providing the details of black spots before the commencement of journey commuters
can be cautious about the black stops well in advance and can drive carefully.
By intimating the commuter about all black spots with in 250mts radius from there location
ensures safety while riding the vehicles.By using current weather and road condition 
along with black spot data, safety of commuter can be extended to further extent with the help of machine learning.
