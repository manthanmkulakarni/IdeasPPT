Building an APP using Artificial Intelligence to reduce the amount of push notifications and email sent by e-commerce apps.
So that only interested user get the notification related to the product/services.
By sending proper notification to the user improper reviews and negative impact on the product can be reduced.
It sends most popular notification among the crowd to new users and based on the 
responce for that custom notification.
Notifications are sent based on the users activity in various e-commerce websites.
Based on daliy activity of user optimal time for sending notification is decided.
By comparing various e-commer site fake notification are blocked.
